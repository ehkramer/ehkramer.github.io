About Me

I am a hands-on mechanical engineer with a cradle to grave philosophy and interests in robotics, human-robot interaction, spaceflight, and astrophysics. Through my educational and professional pursuits I have been able to work in both the fields of engineering design and experimental physics. This combination of experiences has not only allowed me to obtain skills in those two areas, but has also given me the ability to approach tasks with both the practical problem solving ability of an engineer and the unbounded curiosity of a scientist.
